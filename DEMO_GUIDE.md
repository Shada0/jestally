## 📦 Install the Extension

1. Download and extract the ZIP from the link above
2. Open Chrome → `chrome://extensions` → enable **Developer mode**
3. Click **Load unpacked** → select the extracted folder
4. Jestally panel appears on every website (bottom-right)

## 💡 How It Works

**ISL Out (Text/Speech → Signs)**
Type or speak in any of 10 Indian languages → Jestally translates, applies ISL grammar rules, and plays the correct sign sequence in a floating panel on any website.

**ISL In (Signs → Speech)**
Show a hand sign to your webcam → MediaPipe extracts landmarks → custom LSTM model on AWS SageMaker classifies the gesture across 151 ISL signs → result is spoken aloud in your chosen language.
